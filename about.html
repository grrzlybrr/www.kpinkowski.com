Kyler

Origin: Ireland
Meaning: ‘The Protector of People and Good’
My parents were trying to decide between two names (Sean and something else) when this name popped up as they were watching the show American Gladiators. Kyler ‘Superman’ Storm (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Rd7FYS5UzPE) was his full title on the show, and in all honesty, I’m a little perturbed that they didn’t name me Storm. However, I believe that constantly being confused with Kyle and Tyler has taught me a great deal of patience throughout my life, and I wouldn’t be who I am today without my unique name.
