HOME

Hello World!

I’m Kyler Pinkowski and I am currently flying by the seat of my pants as a freelance graphic designer, but my skills and passions encompass everything from Design, Illustration, Painting and Creative Writing, to Coding, Robotics, Biotechnology, Quantum Physics, and basically anything that has to do with obtaining a greater understanding of the world around me. My eccentric personality and creative predisposition stem from my unique name. Similarly, my art is largely driven by my internal qualms and queries, but my fascination with science and technology is excited through existential exploration. I’m always open to learning new things and approaching challenges with unconventional perspectives, so if you want to collaborate or connect feel free to contact me here.
