WRITING

Quill & Ink

Dipped in the ink well of my heart, I spill my soul on the page knowing that even if it resonates with just one person, it is a success. Admittedly, I was skeptical about publishing my poems and stories because of their intimate nature, however, I realized that that was the point. Writing is meant to make us feel naked to the world, not to emphasize our vulnerability, but to illustrate how much we have in common. So, please, read through my depressing break-up poems, my dystopian novel ideas, and everything in between, because you never know what will tug at your heartstrings.
